## Context

### Business Requirements

1. **Sushi variations:** Meat, Seafood, Vegan, Mix
2. **Sizes and pricing:**
   - 8 pieces — £12
   - 16 pieces — £24
   - 20 pieces — £30
   - 30 pieces — £40
   - 50 pieces — £70
3. **24-hour advance ordering:** Customers must order at least 24 hours ahead
4. **Opening hours validation:** Mon-Sat 12pm-6pm, Sun 12pm-5pm
5. **Email notifications:** Send order details to cafe owner via Resend
6. **Payment:** Direct to Revolut Checkout links after form submission

### Technical Decisions

- **Form handling:** Next.js API Route with Resend (not Netlify Forms)
- **State management:** useState for form data and validation
- **Video loading:** IntersectionObserver for lazy loading
- **Email:** Resend SDK with simple HTML template (no React Email for simplicity)
- **Validation:** Client-side with real-time feedback + server-side verification
