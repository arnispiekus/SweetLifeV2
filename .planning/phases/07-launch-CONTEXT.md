# Phase 7: Polish & Launch — Context

## Vision

Skip the SEO blog (AI-generated posts from July 2025, hidden and stale). Focus on making the site feel polished and getting it live.

## How It Works

**Animations:** Framer Motion throughout the site. The feel should match Sweet Life's warm, inviting cafe brand — subtle and smooth rather than flashy. Gentle fade-ins, soft parallax, calming motion that enhances rather than distracts.

**Performance:** Lighthouse audit to catch any issues. The site should load fast and feel responsive.

**SEO:** Proper metadata and structured data for all pages. Local business schema for the cafe.

**Deployment:** Connect to Vercel, auto-deploy on push. Set up domain when ready, DNS switch can happen later.

1. **Framer Motion animations** — Page transitions, scroll reveals, hover states

4. **Structured data** — Local business schema
5. **Vercel deployment** — Connected repo, working preview URLs

7. **Mobile testing** — Everything looks great on phones
